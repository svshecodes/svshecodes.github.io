Introduction

She.codes is a series of coding workshops
for girls to learn cutting-edge topics in programming.

In our 2019 session, learn to create websites and code AI. Through hands-on activities and projects, discover the beauty of computer science while having fun!

The Details

She.codes will hold 2 exciting workshops in March 2019, with coding lessons, a Q&A session with college students and industry professionals working in CS, and free food & prizes.


Location

  • Village Square Library: 4001 Evergreen Village Square, San Jose, CA 95135

Time

  • All workshops are 9 AM - 3 PM.
The Workshops

3/3 Website Development - Beginner

The first workshop, targeted for beginner students, teaches website development using HTML/CSS/JavaScript. As participants learn about programming languages and are introduced to HTML, they can code directly on jsfiddle.net and see their website bloom to life. Once students learn how to use CSS to enhance their website's design and use JS to make their website interactive, they will work on a team project and compete for tech prizes.

3/17 Artificial Intelligence with Python - Advanced

The second workshop, targeted toward students with experience, will teach about artificial intelligence and machine learning, one of the most groundbreaking technologies in the world. After an introduction to AI in the real world, students will dive into the fundamental concepts behind AI algorithms and architecture. Students will learn how to code their own movie recommendation system using an interactive Jupyter notebook. Theory may be challenging, so it is recommended that students have a solid background in Python (functions, classes, and libraries).
